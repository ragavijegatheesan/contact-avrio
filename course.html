LNG Tanker Cargo Operations and Ballast Water Handling Simulator (LCHS) Course.

The aim of this course is to ensure that mariners are trained and prepared to respond safely and efficiently for controlling the cargo handling and other operations on LNG carriers, required in today’s Tanker trade. It gives the participants “hands-on” training and builds their confidence in handling cargo operations and troubleshooting during work in LNG carriers.

Course Focus

The course focuses on ensuring understanding of the principles involved, and the undertaking of all cargo operations carried out on an LNG carrier, in a safe and efficient manner.  

Practical Exercises

Practical exercises structured around the procedures and operation of the cargo and ballast installation of an LNG tanker. 

Operation & Control of Cargo

A safer and more effective contribution to the operation and control of the cargo and ballast installation of a LNG tanker. 

Simulated Cargo Exercises

Simulated cargo exercises conducted using a 'State of the art' LNG Cargo Operations Simulator, covering all subject areas recommended by IMO Model Course 1.36 “Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) Tanker Cargo and Ballast Handling Simulator”.

Key Features

Course Objective

With the growing understanding about hazards involving carriage of LNG cargo, this course develops the professional skills and confidence of Deck officers who are engaged in the handling of Bulk Liquid Cargoes with the help of simulating the cargo operations conducted on LNG Tankers.

Who is it for

Designed for all officers and personnel who will sail on board LNG Carriers, but specifically aimed at those who will be responsible for the safe planning and execution of operations related to the cargo on board such vessels. The course will also be suitable for anybody engaged in the LNG industry who wishes to acquire a better understanding of the cargo operations associated with LNG vessels.
